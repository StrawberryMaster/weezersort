Description

Sorter for Weezer songs. Pick your albums, and hit the Start button.

Click on the song you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.

Depending on how many albums you pick, you'll get up to 700+ picks, so if you need a break, you can save your progress for another time.


Controls

Before sorting

S/Enter: Start sorting
L: Load progress

During sorting

H/Left Arrow: Pick left
L/Right Arrow: Pick right
K/Up Arrow: Tie
J/Down Arrow: Undo
S: Save progress
